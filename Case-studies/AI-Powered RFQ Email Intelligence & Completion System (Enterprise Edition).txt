AI-Powered RFQ Email Intelligence & Completion System (Enterprise Edition)
Overview
Logistics and freight forwarding companies receive a high volume of customer emails requesting freight quotations (RFQs). These emails often arrive with incomplete information, inconsistent formatting, and multiple follow-up conversations before a quotation can be prepared.
As a result, operations teams spend a significant amount of time manually reading emails, requesting missing details, organizing information, and managing long email threads. This creates operational bottlenecks, slows response times, and increases the risk of missed opportunities.
The AI-Powered RFQ Email Intelligence & Completion System is designed to automate the classification, completion, and preparation of RFQ emails before they reach human operators.
The system acts as an intelligent email processing layer that continuously monitors incoming RFQ emails, detects missing information, automatically communicates with customers to gather required details, and delivers fully structured and summarized RFQs to operators for quick pricing decisions.
Objective
The objective of this system is to reduce operational overhead and accelerate quotation turnaround by automating RFQ email classification, information gathering, and response preparation through AI-driven email intelligence.
The solution is designed to:
Automatically monitor incoming RFQ emails across designated inboxes.
Classify RFQ requests using AI based on predefined operational rules.
Detect missing or incomplete shipment details required for pricing.
Automatically send clarification emails to customers requesting missing information.
Continue the email conversation autonomously until all required data points are collected.
Convert unstructured email conversations into structured RFQ summaries.
Deliver complete, ready-to-price RFQs directly to internal operators.
Allow operators to provide pricing responses quickly.
Automatically generate and send professional quotation responses back to customers within the same email thread.
Key Operational Challenges
Logistics companies handling freight quotations often encounter several operational challenges:
High Email Volumes
Freight forwarding teams receive hundreds or even thousands of RFQ emails daily from customers requesting shipment quotations.
Incomplete RFQ Information
Customers frequently submit RFQs without providing all required shipment details such as:
Origin and destination
Cargo dimensions or weight
Shipment type
Delivery timelines
Incoterms
This requires multiple follow-up emails before pricing can begin.
Manual Email Screening
Operations teams must manually read and classify each incoming email to determine whether it is:
A new RFQ
A follow-up
A clarification response
A different type of communication
Fragmented Communication Threads
RFQ conversations often span multiple email exchanges, making it difficult to maintain clear visibility of the full request.
Delayed Response Times
The time required to collect missing details and organize information can delay quotation responses and impact customer satisfaction.
AI-Powered Solution Architecture
The AI-Powered RFQ Email Intelligence & Completion System introduces an intelligent automation layer between incoming customer emails and internal operations teams.
The system continuously monitors incoming emails and processes them using advanced AI models capable of understanding logistics-related content and shipment requirements.
Instead of forwarding every email directly to operators, the system first evaluates whether the RFQ contains all required information.
If critical details are missing, the AI automatically sends clarification requests to the customer and continues the email conversation until the RFQ is complete.
Once all required shipment data is collected, the system generates a structured RFQ summary and forwards it to the operator for pricing.
After the operator provides pricing information, the system automatically formats and sends the quotation response back to the customer within the original email thread.
Core System Capabilities
Intelligent Email Classification
The system automatically analyzes incoming emails and classifies them based on content and context, identifying:
New RFQ requests
RFQ follow-ups
Customer clarification responses
Non-RFQ communications
This ensures that only relevant RFQ-related emails are processed within the quotation workflow.
Automated RFQ Completeness Validation
AI models evaluate each RFQ against predefined data requirements necessary for quotation preparation.
Required data points may include:
Origin location
Destination location
Shipment type (air, sea, road)
Cargo dimensions or weight
Number of packages
Delivery timelines
If required information is missing, the system identifies the gaps automatically.
Autonomous Information Collection
When incomplete RFQs are detected, the system automatically sends professional clarification emails requesting the missing information.
The AI continues the email conversation with the customer, asking follow-up questions until all required shipment details are collected.
This ensures that the RFQ becomes fully structured before reaching internal operators.
Structured RFQ Summarization
Once the required information is collected, the system generates a structured RFQ summary containing all key shipment details.
This summary is delivered to the operator in a clear and concise format, allowing them to review the request quickly without reading the entire email thread.
Operator Pricing Interface
Operators receive only fully completed RFQs that are ready for pricing.
They can simply review the summarized shipment information and enter pricing details without spending time organizing or validating the request.
Automated Quotation Response Generation
Once the operator enters pricing information, the system automatically generates a professional quotation email and sends it to the customer within the original email thread.
This ensures continuity of communication while minimizing manual email drafting.

Faster RFQ Processing
RFQs arrive at the operator stage already structured and complete, enabling faster quotation responses.

Automated Customer Communication

Increased Operator Efficiency

Improved Customer Experience

Conclusion
The AI-Powered RFQ Email Intelligence & Completion System transforms the traditional email-heavy quotation workflow into a structured and automated process.
By intelligently classifying incoming emails, autonomously collecting missing shipment details, and delivering ready-to-price RFQs to operators, the system significantly reduces operational workload while accelerating quotation turnaround times.
This approach enables logistics companies to scale their quotation operations efficiently while maintaining high service quality and faster response times for customers.
