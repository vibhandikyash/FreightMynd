## Platform & Product

### Core Platform Capabilities

**1. AI Agent Automation**
- End-to-end process automation with full delivery and control
- Breaks down complex workflows into coordinated tasks
- Manages sequencing, dependencies, and handoffs from initiation to completion
- Minimal manual intervention

**2. System Integration**
- Integrates with TMS, ERP, WMS, customs platforms, databases, and enterprise apps
- Deep CargoWise integration (Customs Module)
- Real-time bi-directional data consistency across entire tech stack
- Updates flow automatically in both directions

**3. Unified Data Warehouse**
- Transaction-level data captured as single source of truth
- Decision-making processes tracked
- Powers the entire AI stack

**4. Human-in-the-Loop**
- Combines LLMs, transformers, and other AI technologies
- Keeps human experts in control
- Automates tedious/repetitive work

### Solution Breakdown

#### Customs
- Automated customs entry preparation
- Zero-to-minimal touch customs declarations
- AES and ISF filing automation
- Discrepancy detection and flagging
- Data enrichment from internal and external sources
- Integration with customs declaration software (e.g., CargoWise Customs Module)
- **Result:** 70% of customs entries automated, 2x capacity increase

#### Operations
- Automatic pre-alert document processing (BLs, commercial invoices)
- Shipment creation and updates in TMS
- Real-time visibility and accuracy
- ETA tracking
- Booking confirmations
- Operations, customs, finance connected in single workspace

#### Finance
- Zero-touch invoice processing
- Automated AP reconciliation and matching
- Automated approval workflows
- Multi-language, multi-currency, multi-shipment invoice support
- Tax handling, audit trails, payments, exception management
- Centralized dashboards
- Integration with PayCargo and CargoSprint for payments
- **Result:** 75% of invoices processed automatically

#### Payments
- Integrated payment processing
- PayCargo and CargoSprint partnerships
- Settle accounts without leaving the platform
- End-of-month reconciliation automation

#### Warehousing (via OnBoard partnership)
- Warehouse pre-check automation
- Cargo receipt and processing optimization before physical arrival
- **Result:** 3x capacity lift, eliminated human errors

## Competitive Positioning vs FreightMynd

| Dimension | Raft | FreightMynd |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| **Model** | SaaS platform (subscription) | Custom-built systems (client-owned) |
| **Ownership** | Raft-hosted, vendor lock-in | Client-owned, no subscription |
| **Customization** | One-size-fits-many platform | Purpose-built per client TMS/workflows |
| **Pricing** | Custom SaaS pricing | Project-based, no recurring fees |
| **Deployment** | 2 weeks (OnBoard example) | 4–8 weeks |
| **Scale** | 60 customers, 30+ countries | Boutique, enterprise-focused |
| **Funding** | $30M+ VC-backed | Bootstrapped (Bitontree) |
| **Flagship** | Navia, Masterpiece, Scarbrough | Hellmann Worldwide Logistics |
| **TMS depth** | Generic integrations, CargoWise focus | Native per-TMS integration (CW, SAP TM, Oracle, Dynamics, Descartes) |
| **Customs** | Strong — AES/ISF, customs entries | Customs via document intelligence |
| **Finance** | Strong — AP, invoicing, payments | Invoice matching, overcharge detection |
| **Operations** | Pre-alerts, booking, tracking | Full document pipeline, quote automation |
| **Differentiator** | Scale, breadth, enterprise SaaS | Depth, ownership, custom edge cases |

## Opportunities for FreightMynd

1. **Ownership narrative** — Raft is VC-backed SaaS with vendor lock-in; FreightMynd is client-owned
2. **TMS depth** — Raft's TMS integration is generic; FreightMynd builds native per-instance
3. **Edge case handling** — Raft is one-size-fits-many; FreightMynd builds for your specific suppliers/formats
4. **SEO gap** — Raft has minimal SEO presence (Framer CSR site, no keyword pages, no glossary, no comparison pages)
5. **Pricing transparency** — Raft is opaque custom pricing; FreightMynd is project-based with no recurring fees
6. **Middle East / regional content** — Raft has no regional targeting pages
7. **Quote management** — Raft focuses on ops/customs/finance; FreightMynd has autonomous quote management
8. **Document intelligence depth** — Raft claims automation %; FreightMynd has detailed technical architecture (LangGraph, OCR pipeline, self-learning supplier onboarding)
